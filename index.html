Welcome to A.S. WordSmiths, your one-stop solution to all of your editing needs.

We here at A.S. WordSmiths believe that choice is king, so we have decided to give you the freedom of choice. As such, we offer a wide variety of editing services to suit your particular needs, ranging from basic proofreading to all-encompassing macro editing. All of our packages are charged on a per-word, or per-page, basis. This ensures that when you select your ideal editing package, you'll be charged for only what you need done, and not a cent more.

Take a look at our editing packages below. Once you're satisfied that you've made the correct choice, head on over to our Pricing & Submissions page to use our brand new pricing calculator. Our Editing Services

Proofreading

Our proofreading service allows you to get honest feeedback on the manuscript you're writing. Also known as beta-reading, we can offer you unbiased opinions, views, and advice, to ensure that your manuscript is the best that it can be.

Line Editing

All writers make mistakes. This can range from typos, to punctuation errors, to grammar mistakes. Luckily, we're around to help you fix those mistakes! Accidental double space? Fixed. Accidentally used a question mark in place of a comma? Fixed. Sentence doesn't make sense grammatically? Fixed! We'll fix anything that falls under basic spelling, punctuation, or grammar, ensuring that your manuscript conveys professionalism.

Simple Editing

Our simple editing package is the best of both worlds. Need only basic line editing and proofreading, as opposed to the full range that our complex editing package offers? Then simple editing is for you. We will ensure that all standard grammar, punctuation, and spelling errors are corrected, as well as offering constructive criticism and an honest look as objective readers. Enjoy a discount on your proofreading by taking this combined package!

Complex Editing

Our complex editing package includes anything and everything, formatting aside, that a writer could ever possibly need, from basic line editing corrections, to proofreading, to bigger - story changing, even - macro edits. This package ensures that all mistakes will be corrected, ranging from the basics, all the way to the big stuff, like consistency, sentence structure, repetition, and more! This extremely comprehensive package will ensure that your manuscript receives the ultimate in editing service.

Formatting

So, your manuscript is done and edited, but something still doesn't look right. Well, that's what we're here for! We'll make sure that any and all formatting is fully consistent and up to standard from the start to the end of your manuscript, as well as ensuring that your book is kindle-ready, should you choose to self-publish. This option is great for visual novels, as well as manuscripts where formatting is of the utmost importance.

Complex Editing & Formatting

The true be-all end-all of our offerings. With this package you can expect everything from both our complex editing package, as well as our formatting package, all in one. You'll also be able to enjoy a 50% discount on the formatting for taking this fully complete package.

Other Services

Transcription

Ever recorded something and had to tackle the long arduous process of transcribing it piece by piece? Well no more! We here at A.S. WordSmiths are more than happy to take that burden off your hands. We work quickly and accurately to ensure a perfectly transcribed document, with no errors, word for word.

Web Content Writing

Ever had a website built, but had no idea what to write? We've all been there, struggling to come up with the right words, or even any words at all. Well, that's where we come in. We're only too happy to have a look at your website and your needs and write all of your website content so that you don't have to. Make running a website quick and painless with A.S. WordSmiths!

Blurb Writing

So you've written an amazing work of art, but you have no idea how to describe it. Well, leave that up to us! We'll give your book a read and come up with a blurb that makes your published work nothing short of appealing to your readers! Best combined with our proofreading package, for an unbiased view on your manuscript alongside the blurb.
